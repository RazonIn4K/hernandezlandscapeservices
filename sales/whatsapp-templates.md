## NOTES FOR EXECUTION

1. **Timing Matters:** Send during business hours (10 AM - 5 PM local). Avoid Mondays (too busy) and Fridays after 2 PM (weekend mode).

2. **Attachment Order:** Lead with visuals (infographic) in Template #1. Save PDF for Template #2 follow-up.

3. **Voice:** Keep it conversational, no corporate jargon. Use his name. Use emojis sparingly (1-2 per message max).

4. **Link Hygiene:** Always test Stripe links before pasting. Use URL shortener if they're too long (bit.ly, tinyurl).

5. **Read Receipts:** Track when he opens messages. If he reads but doesn't reply for 6+ hours, that's your signal to send Template #2 next day.

6. **Personalization:** Reference specific services he offers (lawn, tree, landscaping) in follow-ups to show you understand his business.

7. **Exit Strategy:** After Template #3 gets no reply, STOP. Wait 30 days. Never send more than 3 unreturned messages in a row.
