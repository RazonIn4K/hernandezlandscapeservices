# SEO Fixes Checklist: Hernandez Landscape & Tree Service LLC

This checklist prioritizes technical and content fixes based on their potential impact on local search visibility and revenue.

## Priority 0: Critical Revenue Blockers (Implement Immediately)

- [x] **Create Dedicated Service Pages**: 
  - Build `tree-removal/index.html` targeting "Tree Removal DeKalb IL".
  - Build `lawn-care/index.html` targeting "Lawn Care DeKalb IL".
  - Build `snow-removal/index.html` targeting "Snow Removal DeKalb IL".
  - *Impact*: Captures high-intent, bottom-of-funnel search traffic that currently bounces or goes to competitors with dedicated pages.

- [x] **Upgrade Schema Markup on Homepage**:
  - Change `@type` from `LocalBusiness` to `HomeAndConstructionBusiness`.
  - Add `FAQPage` schema with 5 common local questions.
  - Add `BreadcrumbList` schema.
  - *Impact*: Increases eligibility for rich snippets in SERPs, improving click-through rates.

- [x] **Update XML Sitemap**:
  - Add the three new service pages to `sitemap.xml` with `priority=0.9`.
  - Update `lastmod` dates accurately.
  - *Impact*: Ensures Google discovers and indexes the new revenue-driving pages quickly.

## Priority 1: High Impact (Implement Soon)

- [x] **Fix Open Graph Tags**:
  - Change `og:type` from `website` to `business.business` in `index.html`.
  - *Impact*: Improves how the site appears when shared on social media, building local trust.

- [x] **Internal Linking Strategy**:
  - Update homepage service cards to link to the new dedicated service pages instead of just the quote form.
  - *Impact*: Distributes page authority and helps Google understand the site's topical relevance.

## Priority 2: Medium Impact (Ongoing Maintenance)

- [ ] **NAP Citation Cleanup**:
  - Audit and standardize listings on Yelp, BBB, and local DeKalb directories to exactly match the GBP (1029 Lewis St, DeKalb, IL 60115 | 815-501-1478).
  - *Impact*: Builds local entity trust with Google, crucial for Map Pack rankings.

- [ ] **Review Generation Strategy**:
  - Implement a system to request reviews from satisfied customers, as the GBP currently lacks reviews.
  - *Impact*: Reviews are a primary ranking factor for the Local Pack and heavily influence conversion rates.
